## Blog post structure (template)

1. **Headline** — exact keyword in natural phrasing
2. **Opening para** — lead with the reader's problem, not "in this article"
3. **Body** — 1500–2500 words, 6–10 H2 sections, real photos every 3–4 sections
4. **FAQ section** — 5+ Q&A entries (triggers FAQPage schema)
5. **Related products block** — 3 products from `blog_post_products` pivot
6. **Author block** — Mona's photo + WhatsApp CTA
7. **Related posts** — 3 links
