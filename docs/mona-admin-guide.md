Log in with your email and password.

---

## Daily tasks

### Check new orders
1. Click **Orders** in the left sidebar
2. New orders appear with a green "New" badge
3. Click an order to see the customer's details and sizing photo
4. Call or WhatsApp the customer within 1 hour to confirm

### Mark payment as verified
1. Open the order
2. Scroll to "Payment Proof" — you'll see the image the customer uploaded
3. Check your JazzCash / bank / EasyPaisa for the transfer
4. Once confirmed: click **Verify Payment** → status moves to "Confirmed"

### Move an order through production
- Drag the order card across the kanban board:
  `New → Confirmed → In Production → Shipped → Delivered`
- Or open the order and change the status from the dropdown.

---

## Managing products

### Add a new product
1. Go to **Products** → **New Product**
2. Fill in: Name, Description, Price (in PKR, numbers only — no ₨ symbol), Tier
3. Upload your photos (the first one becomes the cover)
4. Toggle "Active" to ON when ready to publish
5. Click **Save**

### Temporarily hide a product
Open the product → toggle **Active** to OFF → Save.
The product disappears from the shop but is not deleted.

## Blog (coming in Phase 4)

- Go to **Blog** → **New Post**
- Write your content in the editor
- Fill in: Meta Title, Meta Description, Target Keyword
- Upload a cover photo
- Set **Publish Date** → the post goes live automatically at that time

## Settings to update regularly

Go to **Settings** in the sidebar:

- **Payment accounts** — update JazzCash / EasyPaisa / bank number if they change
- **Business hours** — shown on the Contact page
- **Shipping rates** — PKR amount per city tier
- **WhatsApp number** — used in all site CTAs
