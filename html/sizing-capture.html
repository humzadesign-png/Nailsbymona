Nails by Mona Need help?

Take 2 close-up photos.

One for your fingers, one for your thumb. Place a coin above the nail row in each photo so we can measure scale. About 90 seconds.

1

Lay your hand flat on a dark surface

Any dark cloth or surface works — it helps us read the outline of your nails clearly.

2

Place a coin above your nails

Any coin works — Rs. 5, Rs. 10, or any coin you have. Keep it flat and clearly visible.

3

Hold your phone straight overhead

Shoot straight down so your nails fill the guide frame. Tap the shutter when the border turns green.
