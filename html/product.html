The Dusty Rose Ombre is one of those sets that feels effortlessly put-together. The gradient builds from a sheer, almost skin-like base at the nail bed to a full dusty blush at the tip — soft enough for every day, polished enough for meetings and events. The tone sits somewhere between rose and mauve, which means it photographs warmly under most lighting and complements a wide range of skin tones.

I build each nail individually on a form. Gel base, then the ombre blend — which involves loading two colours on a brush and feathering them across the nail surface while the gel is still workable. I cure each layer under the lamp before the next one goes on. The topcoat is a high-shine gel finish that seals everything and gives that just-done salon look.

What's in the box: 10 nails (sized to your measurements) + 2 spare nails + brush-on glue + prep pad + application guide card. Packed in tissue, boxed. Lead time: 5–9 working days from sizing confirmation. Every order is quality-checked by me before it leaves.

Already ordered before?

I keep your measurements on file. Just mention it when you order and I'll use your saved profile — no re-measuring needed.

  • Apply on clean, dry nails — use the prep pad included. Don't skip this step; it's 80% of how long they last.

  • Press firmly for 30 seconds from base to tip, working out air bubbles. Hold, don't just press.

  • Avoid soaking in water for the first hour after application — the glue needs time to fully cure.

  • To remove: soak in warm water for 10–15 minutes, then gently lift from the side. Never force or peel — that damages the natural nail.

  • Store in the original box. With careful removal, you'll get 3–5 wears from this set.
